Problem 3

To play a perfect prank on their friend Fred, Zack and Cody need to find a perfect number. A perfect number is a positive integer that is equal to the sum of its proper positive divisors, that is, the sum of its positive divisors excluding the number itself. For example 6 is a perfect number as 1 + 2 + 3 = 6.
First line of input contains T, the number of test cases. Next T lines contain a number N. For every N you have to output the sum of its proper positive divisors, In the next line, print ‘Yes’ if it is a perfect number, else print ‘No’.
Given: 1 ≤ N ≤ 263, 1 ≤ T ≤ 105

Sample Input

2
6
12

Sample Output

6
Yes
16
No
